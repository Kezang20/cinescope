Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use CineScope.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information: Only what you provide during login (like username or email).
  • Local data: Information stored in your browser using Local Storage (watchlist or login status).
  • Usage data: Pages visited, searches, and interactions with the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve CineScope features.
  • To save your watchlist and preferences.
  • To enhance your browsing experience on the app.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties. Movie data shown in CineScope comes from TMDb API.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

CineScope uses Local Storage to remember your session and watchlist. No tracking cookies or third-party trackers are used.

5. Your Rights

You can clear your browser data anytime to remove stored information. You can stop using the app at any time if you are concerned about privacy.
